The Best Movies According to Alvaro

My top 3 movies of all-time.

All the President's Men (1976)

This is one of my favourite movies. In the early 1970s, Washington Post reporters Bob Woodward and Carl Bernstein uncover the Watergate scandal - a conspiracy to cover up abuses of power leading all the way to the Oval Office and eventually to the resignation of President Richard Nixon.

Blade Runner (1982)

This is one of my science fiction movies. A blade runner must pursue and terminate four replicants who stole a ship in space and have returned to Earth to find their creator.
